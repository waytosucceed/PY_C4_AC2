Activity 1: Discount Calculator

You went to a shopping mall. While billing you get to know about certain discounts. The cashier says if your bill is greater than 500 you get a discount! But, there are 2 criteria for discount, if you have a membership card you get 20% discount and if you do not have a membership card you get 10% discount. Keeping this in mind you need to write a code to input the total bill and if they have a membership card. Accordingly, you need to display the final amount!

Activity 2: Vote Eligibility Checker

In the last class, we created a vote eligibility checker where we gave the condition that if the age is greater than or equal to 18 you can vote. Do you think that is the only condition for voting eligibility? There is another condition: the person should be a citizen. Can you modify the code to check if a person is eligible to vote by taking input of age and whether they are a citizen or not?
